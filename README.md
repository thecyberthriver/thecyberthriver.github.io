# The Cyber Thriver 🌟

Welcome to **The Cyber Thriver**! This project is dedicated to empowering women and underrepresented groups in the cybersecurity field. It provides valuable resources, including tech conference details, upskilling opportunities, certifications, and personal branding tips to help individuals succeed in this dynamic industry.

---

## 🌐 Live Website
You can explore the live site here:  
[The Cyber Thriver](https://thecyberthriver.github.io/The-Cyber-Thriver)

---

## 📋 Features

- **Cybersecurity Conferences**: A curated list of conferences to attend, complete with dates, locations, and links.
- **Upskilling Opportunities**: Access free and paid training programs from top companies.
- **Certifications Guide**: Discover certifications based on your experience level in cybersecurity.
- **Personal Branding**: Learn how to showcase your cybersecurity knowledge effectively on social media.
- **Stock Photos**: Featuring inspiring images of Black women thriving in cybersecurity.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Jekyll**: A static site generator for building the website.
- **GitHub Pages**: For hosting the project.
- **Markdown**: For content management.
- **Ruby & Bundler**: For managing Jekyll dependencies.
